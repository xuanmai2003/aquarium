SUMMER DAY CAMP (AGES 5-13)

Children will have the opportunity to immerse themselves in our aquarium world through a combination of hands-on indoor and outdoor activities, gallery exploration, and even behind the scenes adventures!

REGISTRATION TEMPORARILY ON HOLD

Please contact camps@ripleysaquariumofcanada.com for any inquiries

GENERAL INFORMATION

AGE
5-13 years. Must be at least 5 years of age at the START of camp.

HOURS
Regular Camp Hours: 9am-4pm
Extended Camp Hours: 8am-9am and 4-6pm

PRICE
$325 /week + HST
$260 /week + HST for annual pass holders*
$50 /week + HST for extended care

*Annual pass holders must be part of the child’s immediate family. Annual pass must be valid at time of camp in order to receive the discount.

SUMMER DAY CAMP 2020 SESSION DATES

The health and safety of campers, staff, and families is our top priority. For the past several months, our team has worked hard to determine whether we could safely offer summer camps in July and August, while adapting our programs to ensure physical distancing and adding health screening and protective measures for our campers and staff.

With this in mind, we have made the very difficult decision to cancel our planned 2020 summer camps. We are still exploring options for alternative in-person programming and/or a virtual camp program for later this summer. We will continue to follow guidance from public health officials as we develop these new experiences and share details with you as soon as we can.
