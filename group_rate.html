GROUP RATES 10+

group reservations

At Nexus Aquarium we offer reduced rates for groups of 10 people or more.
Reservations are required. Groups are recommended to book a week prior to the scheduled arrival date. However, depending on availability, last-minute requests may be accommodated.
Please note, your reservation is not confirmed until we have provided a formal invoice.

request a reservation

Please download the reservation form for your group size/type and email it to Groups@RipleysAquariumofCanada.com.
A Group Sales Coordinator will confirm receipt of your reservation form and provide a formal invoice within 2-3 business days.
Full payment is required with final numbers three days before scheduled visit.

SAFETY MEASURES

For the health and safety of our Guests and Employees, all ticket holders agree to:
  • Wear a mask/face covering that covers the mouth, nose and chin for the duration of your visit per City of Toronto bylaw.
  • Wash/sanitize hands before entry and throughout the experience.
  • Social distance from Guests and Employees while touring the Aquarium.
  • Spread out while viewing the exhibits and not backtrack through the tour.
  • Cover coughs and sneezes.
  • Not proceed with entering the Aquarium if you /your group:
    1. Have or had Covid-19 related symptoms in the past 14 days.
    2. Have or come in contact with someone that has Covid-19 related symptoms in the past 14 days.
    3. Travelled outside of Canada in the past 14 days.

GROUP POLICIES & INFO

*One complimentary admission for every 20 paying visitors (entrance only). Complimentary admission for bus driver when requested in advance.

Supervisor Ratio REQUIRED for Child/Youth Groups: 1:3 (0-2 yrs.), 1:4 (3-8 yrs.), 1:8 (9-13 yrs.), 1:15 (15-16 yrs.). Complimentary admission for Ratio Staff EXCEPT when supervising children aged 2 & under.

Group tickets are non-refundable. Unused group tickets can be exchanged for a timed ticket only.

All prices and policies are subject to change without notice.

Arrival & Departure
Upon arrival, please have your group members line up on the ramp, located on the southeast side of the building. One group representative may enter the building to check in at Guest Services to collect your ticket envelope. Once the tickets have been collected, please distribute the access to your group and have them enter, allowing social distancing spacing. We highly recommend you split your group into smaller bubble groups while touring the Aquarium.

Length of Visit
Self-guided tours take approximately two hours to complete.

Bus Loading & Parking
Tour and school buses may use the loading zone located in front of Ripley’s Aquarium of Canada (north side of Bremner Boulevard) to drop off and pick up passengers. Bus parking is available on the south side of Bremner Boulevard across from the Rogers Centre for approximately $40.
